About me

Hey There! So you have reached this far. Now let me introduce myself.

When the clock gently crawls past midnight and the whole world goes to sleep, a silent demon in me wakes up to give birth to new stories from the dead old memories. Hi! I am an Engineer by profession and a story teller by occasion. I write codes duing the days and quotes during the nights. The tryst of memories and emotions write my stories.

Originally from the city of joy, Kolkata, India, I spent 23 years of my life with my loving parents and elder brother. I moved to Tech in Fall 2018 to the beautiful neighbourhood of Houghton, MI, for graduate studies. I am a sociable, ambivert yet friendly Homo Sapiens. And most importantly, I do not Byte if one says 'Hi!'.
